Small Meals

Takoyaki

You need:

  • Batter
  • Octopus (of course)
  • Dashi Powder
  • Soy Sauce
  • Bonito Flakes
  • Pickled Ginger
  • Tempura Scraps
  • Green Onions
  • Seaweed
  • Takoyaki Sauce
  • Takoyaki Pan

Step by Step:

  1. Prepare takoyaki pan
  2. Once done preparing, cook the takoyaki
  3. Use chopsticks to flip the takoyaki
  4. Cook for 4-5 minutes
  5. When you're finished cooking, put seawood, condiments, etc. on takoyaki and enjoy

Tempura

You need:

  • Shrimp
  • Tempura Batter
  • Deep pan/Frying Pan
  • Oil

Step by Step:

  1. Prepare the frying pan and put oil
  2. Coat the shrimp with the batter
  3. Once you're done coating the tempura/shrimp, start frying
  4. Cook for 2-3 minutes
  5. Once done frying, drain the oil and enjoy

Meals

Katsudon

You need:

  • Boneless porkchop/tonkatsu
  • Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Egg, Flour, Breadcrumbs
  • Rice
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Onions
  • Rice Bowl
  • Frying Pan

Step by Step:

  1. Prepare the chicken/porkchop
  2. Dip the chicken/porkchop in the Egg>Flour>Breadcrumbs
  3. Heat the pan with oil
  4. Cook the onions for 2 minutes
  5. Place the chicken/porkchop on the pan and cook
  6. Cook for 3-5 minutes
  7. Once done cooking, transfer to a rice bowl
  8. Place onions, soy sauce, condiments, and etc.
  9. Enjoy

Desserts

Mochi

You need:

  • Glutinous Rice
  • Cornstarch
  • 1/4 Sugar
  • 3/4 Water

Step by Step:

  1. Mix the glutinous rice, sugar, and water
  2. Microwave for 1 minute
  3. Steam for 10-15 minutes
  4. Shape mochi
  5. Dust it with the cornstarch and enjoy

Matcha Swiss Roll

You need:

  • 4 Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Matcha
  • Whipping Cream

Step by Step:

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Make the batter
  3. Put the matcha in the batter and mix
  4. Transfer the batter in a pan
  5. Bake for 10 minutes
  6. Once done baking, smear whipping cream and roll the cake and enjoy

Japanese Cheesecake

Step by Step:

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Make the batter
